# Trader Performance Analysis using Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

## Project Overview

This project analyzes cryptocurrency trader performance by combining historical trading data with the Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index. The objective is to understand how market sentiment influences trading behavior, profitability, and decision-making.

---

## Objective

- Analyze trader performance across different market sentiment conditions.
- Compare profitability, win rate, trade frequency, and trade size.
- Identify trader behavior patterns.
- Provide actionable recommendations supported by data.

---

## Dataset

### 1. Historical Trader Data
Contains:
- Trader Account
- Execution Price
- Trade Size (USD)
- Trade Direction
- Closed PnL
- Trading Fee
- Timestamp

### 2. Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index
Contains:
- Date
- Fear & Greed Score
- Sentiment Classification

## Key Findings

- Highest win rate was observed during Extreme Greed market conditions.
- Traders executed the largest number of trades during Fear periods.
- Average trade size was highest during Fear sentiment.
- Increased trading activity did not necessarily improve trading performance.
- Market sentiment significantly influenced trader behavior.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Incorporate market sentiment into trading strategies.
- Focus on trade quality rather than trade quantity.
- Apply dynamic position sizing during volatile market conditions.
